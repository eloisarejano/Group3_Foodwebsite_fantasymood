WAGYU BEEF

Wagyu beef from Japan is the most priced beef in the world. High-grade wagyu can cost up to $200 per pound. The rarest steak in the world, olive wagyu, can cost anywhere from $120 to over $300 for a steak. Wagyu calves can be 40 times the price of US cattle

IBERIAN HAM

Iberian Ham, or Jamon Iberico, is one of the most expensive meats in the world. A leg of it can cost as much as as $4,500. It is made from the rear leg of the black Iberian pig, a rare breed that can be found in the southern and western portion of the Iberian Peninsula, which comrises Spain and Portugal.
