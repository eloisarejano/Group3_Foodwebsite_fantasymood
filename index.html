ALMAS CAVIAR

Almas caviar has been enjoyed as a delicacy back as far as the ancient Greeks who imported it form the area now known as Crimea in the southern Ukraine. According to the Guinness Book of World Records, Almas Caviar is regularly sold for $34,500 USD per kilogram, making it the most expensive food in the world.
